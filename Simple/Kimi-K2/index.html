Frequently Asked Questions

How accurate is the accessibility data? +

Our data combines municipal records, community reports, and on-site verification. Information is updated daily and verified by local accessibility advocates.

How do I report incorrect information? +

You can report issues directly through the app by tapping "Report Problem" on any location. Our community review process typically updates information within 24-48 hours.

Does it work offline? +

Yes! You can download maps and routes for offline use. Voice navigation and accessibility alerts work even without an internet connection.

How is my privacy protected? +

We don't track your precise location or store personal data. All navigation happens locally on your device, and uploaded photos are automatically processed to blur identifying information.

Will it drain my battery? +

We've optimized for battery efficiency with smart location sampling and offline-first design. Typical usage adds less than 5% battery drain per hour.
