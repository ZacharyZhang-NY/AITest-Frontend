Frequently Asked Questions

How accurate is the accessibility data?

Our data combines official city records with community validations. Each location requires at least 3 user confirmations within the last 30 days. We achieve 95% accuracy for major routes and 88% for secondary paths.

How do I report incorrect information?

Use the in-app report button or shake your device while on a route. You can submit photos, text descriptions, or quick-tap predefined issues. Reports are reviewed within 24 hours.

Does the app work offline?

Yes! Download area packs up to 50MB each for offline navigation. The app caches your frequently used routes automatically. Offline mode includes all accessibility data but excludes real-time updates.

How is my privacy protected?

Location data is only used for navigation and never stored on servers. Photos automatically blur faces and license plates. All data is encrypted and you can delete your contribution history anytime.

Will this drain my battery?

The app uses smart location updates that adjust frequency based on your speed. On average, it uses 15% less battery than standard navigation apps. Enable battery saver mode for even lower consumption.
